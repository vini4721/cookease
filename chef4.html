Chef Kunal Kapoor

Chef Kunal Kapur, a celebrated Indian chef and television personality, is renowned for his innovative approach to traditional Indian cuisine. Among his signature creations is the Phool Surprise, a dessert that artfully combines a chocolate flower with a delectable blueberry cheesecake. This dish exemplifies Chef Kapur's flair for blending artistic presentation with rich flavors.

Phool Surprise

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 1 balloon (inflated to a small size)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 200g cream cheese, softened
  • 100g powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 100g fresh blueberries
  • 200ml heavy cream

Recipe:To create Chef Kunal Kapur's Phool Surprise, start by melting dark chocolate until smooth and glossy. Grease a small balloon with vegetable oil, dip it into the chocolate to coat evenly, and refrigerate it until the chocolate hardens. Once set, deflate the balloon carefully to reveal a delicate chocolate flower shell. For the cheesecake filling, blend fresh blueberries with sugar to make a puree. In a separate bowl, beat softened cream cheese with powdered sugar and vanilla extract until creamy, then fold in whipped cream for a light texture. Mix in the blueberry puree for a vibrant flavor. Assemble the dessert by spooning the cheesecake filling into the chocolate flower shell and garnish with fresh blueberries, mint leaves, and a drizzle of blueberry or chocolate sauce. Serve as a stunning, delicious dessert that blends artistry with taste.
